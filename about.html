About Abdoulaye

Born and raised in Africa, Abdoulaye moved to California over a decade ago to pursue his career and passions. Currently working for Tesla Inc., Abdoulaye balances his time between his professional life and his love for fashion, cars, and family. His integrity, generosity, and deep connection to his family and friends make him a beloved figure in his community.
